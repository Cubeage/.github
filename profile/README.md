## What we build

**Cubeage** publishes **premium board and card games** — mahjong, Big 2, and related classics — with **authentic Hong Kong & Taiwan rules**, live multiplayer, and production craft.

| Series | Intent |
|--------|--------|
| **Fun** | Fast social tables — competitive, light session |
| **Tycoon** | Progression, characters, seasons — deeper empires on real rules |

> Rules first. Craft always. Free to start.
